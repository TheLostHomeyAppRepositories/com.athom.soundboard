Upload your own sounds (mp3 and wav are supported) to Homey, and play them from any Flow!

To upload your own sounds, install the App and navigate to the app's Advanced Settings.

MP3 and WAV files up to 900 KB can be uploaded. Play sounds your own Flows, or connect with other apps such as Sonos to use this app as your own sound library.
